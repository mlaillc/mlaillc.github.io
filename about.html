Who We Are

MLAI LLC builds human-end-user-focused, local-first, privacy-focused, personalized AI experiences. Intense focus on individual end users. Based in Bellevue, WA.

Founded by Anand Ramanathan (RC), a developer and applied AI scientist with 20+ years of experience - founding-team Microsoft BizTalk Server, Microsoft AutoML, Amazon, Ripcord, and independent ventures. Listed on Toptal as a leading applied scientist in LLM/GPT applications. Daily power user of Claude Code at the super-expert level.

Our products are built on a foundation of personalization for the human at the keyboard, local-first AI processing (with hosted models when the task demands it), privacy by design, and a track record of independently arriving at ideas before they became mainstream.
