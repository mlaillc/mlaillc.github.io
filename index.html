How we build

Core principles

👤

Personalized · end-user focused

Focus on the end user - whether at work, at home, or as a parent.

💻

Local-first AI

On-device inference where it works (Apple MLX, Ollama, local LLMs); hosted models when the task demands it. Your data stays on your machine by default.

🔒

Privacy by default

No ad telemetry. No accounts that extract you. Tools that respect that you own your data and your attention.

⚡

Often early

Twenty years of independently arriving at ideas before they became mainstream - no-code, visual knowledge graphs, multi-LLM councils, AI second brains.
